Welcome to Akemi's Anime World: Reviewing anime you've never heard of since 1998. Which is so long ago half of you whippersnappers probably weren't even born yet. Back then we had monitors the size of your backpack with screens the size of your iPod's, and they had 256 colors if we were lucky, and we liked it. Oh, yeah, and anime came on VHS tapes with two episodes each that cost more than dinner at a fancy restaurant and you felt lucky if someone even bothered to translate it instead of just making crap up for the subtitles.
